Esolang Playground

The Unofficial IDE of Esolang, the esoteric programming languages wiki

What is the Esolang Playground?

Esolang Playground is the unofficial IDE of Esolang, the esoteric programming languages wiki. It is created by InfiniteDonuts, an esoteric language hobbyist. It is still a work in progress as some of the more popular languages are still being implemented.

What languages are supported?

Currently, only a few small languages are supported: L, brainflop, miniBrainflop, and brainf**k. Work is in progress on 0587 and Befunge support.
